Summary

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" is a personal finance and self-help book written by Robert T. Kiyosaki. The book, published in 1997, contrasts the financial philosophies and practices of two father figures in the author's life: his biological father (referred to as "Poor Dad") and the father of his childhood best friend (referred to as "Rich Dad").
